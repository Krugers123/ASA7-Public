# ASA 7 What It Is / Is Not

## Purpose

This document clarifies the public-safe identity of ASA 7.

- a structural signature framework for advanced AI trajectories
- a repeatability-focused instability analysis surface
- a disciplined comparison layer for micro-cracks, threshold transitions, and recovery behavior

## What ASA 7 Is Not

- a claim of metaphysical proof
- a one-output anomaly gallery

- a declaration about the ultimate nature of reality

- the claim gets bigger than the evidence
- the language gets looser than the method
- one strange result replaces repeated structure

ASA 7 is strongest when it remains anchored in:

- reruns
- controls
- probe families
- repeatable fracture behavior
- public-safe technical language
